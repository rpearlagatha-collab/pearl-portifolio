My name is Kwikiriza Pearl Agatha

Iam a registered Midwife with 10 years of experience in maternal and child health.

I believe a mother and her family should experience a good pregnancy labour and postpartum journey

I am Pearl Agatha — a registered midwife, mother,partner, advocate, and founder of Mother Mentor Uganda. My decade in maternal and child health has taught me one thing above all: when a mother is truly supported, the ripple goes far. I work at bedside and in the community — providing clinical midwifery care,am a Midwife leader too, mentoring young mothers through practical life skills, and using my voice to advocate for better maternal health outcomes across Uganda. Rooted in Kigata village in Sheema District, connected to communities across the country — I bring my faith, my culture, and my full heart into everything I do for mothers.
